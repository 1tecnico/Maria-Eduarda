Fighting Styles

Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport that uses only punches to attack and defend. It is known for its strategy, agility, and endurance.

MMA

MMA (Mixed Martial Arts) is a sport that combines various fighting techniques such as boxing, jiu-jitsu, muay thai, and wrestling, promoting complete and versatile combat.

Jiu-Jitsu

Jiu-Jitsu is a martial art that emphasizes ground fighting, using leverage, chokes, and submissions to control the opponent.
